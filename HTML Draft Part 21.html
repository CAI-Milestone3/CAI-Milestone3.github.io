Energy and Space in the UAE

 

The UAE has already made plans regarding space and energy.It also has executed a space and energy plan previously like  Masdar City (Masdar City's master plan is fundamental to the city's sustainability goals, powered by renewable energy and it is through the reduction of demand for energy and water.It also uses sunlight and converts into a source of energy.

The Emirates Mars Mission is a United Arab Emirates Space Agency uncrewed space exploration mission to Mars. The Hope orbiter was launched on 19 July 2020.
In March 2019, the UAE government launched the National Space Strategy 2030, which sets the general framework for the UAE's space industry and activities carried out by public and private sectors for the years leading up to 2030.
UAE Energy Plan for 2050 is a strategy that targets an energy mix that combines renewable, nuclear and clean energy sources to meet the UAE's economic requirements and environmental goals.There are many other futuristic some in the near future(like a plan due 2021) and others in the far future(like a plan due 2071 and 2117).
UAE's economic requirements and environmental goals for the 2050 Plan:
  • 38 per cent gas
  • 44 per cent clean energy
  • 12 per cent clean coal
  • 6 per cent nuclear
