UAE's Past And History

The United Arab Emirates is situated in the Middle East and southwest Asia while bordering Oman and Saudi Arabia.Abu Dhabi is the capital of UAE, and it is the country's largest and the second most populous city.The UAE consists of seven emirates and was founded on 2 December 1971 as a federation. Six of the seven emirates (Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Umm Al Quwain and Fujairah) combined on that date. The seventh, Ras al Khaimah, joined the federation on 10 February 1972.Before its re-creation as the United Arab Emirates in 1971, the UAE was known as the Trucial States, a collection of sheikhdoms extending from the Straits of Hormuz to the west along the Persian Gulf.The UAE is home to over 200 nationalities. Emiratis constitute roughly 20% of the total population, making UAE home to one of the world's highest percentage of immigrants.UAE used petrol and oil as a souce of income and energy(but the denpendance on it decreased with time).The UAE also didn't have a space program until the past few years.
